Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect
We collect your name, email, username, and activity data (articles read, comments, coins, bets, withdrawals). Payments are handled by Paystack — we never store card details.
2. How We Use Your Data
Your data is used to operate your account, calculate rewards, process withdrawals, and send updates. We do not sell your data to third parties.
3. Coins & Earnings
Coins are virtual rewards convertible to Naira per admin's current rate. NewsEarn reserves the right to adjust rates. Fraudulent activity results in permanent bans.
4. Casino & Gambling
Casino games use coins only — no real money. You must be 18+ to use the Casino Arena. The coupon game deducts 0.5 coins per play regardless of result.
5. Data Security
Data is stored via Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
6. Contact
For privacy questions, use our Support Chat. We respond within 48 hours.
